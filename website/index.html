DRAWING VERIFICATION

Check drawings against the 3D model before they reach the floor.

Upload a drawing. InspectorPro compares it to the CAD model feature by feature — missing dimensions, tolerance gaps, incorrect views, uncalled-out holes. Each feature is identified from the 3D model, not inferred from the drawing. Results land in a findings table you can act on immediately.

REVISION COMPARISON

See exactly what changed between revisions, down to the feature level.

Load Rev A and Rev B side by side. Removed features light up red, added green, modified amber. InspectorPro uses the CAD model’s feature identity — not a pixel overlay — so it can tell you a specific flange was extended from 44.45mm to 76.2mm, not just that “something moved.”

DEVIATION ANALYSIS

Make scrap, rework, or accept decisions with assembly-aware reasoning.

When a dimension is out of tolerance, InspectorPro doesn’t just flag it. It checks the assembly — mate relationships, fit interfaces, downstream parts — and tells you whether the part will still function, what risk you carry if you accept it, and what to do next. Engineering reasoning, not just a red cell.

FAI REPORTING

Generate first article reports without rebuilding the job in spreadsheets.

Numbered balloons on the drawing. Auto pass/fail evaluation as you type measurements. Enter “all bores measured 25.003” and eight cells fill at once. AI-generated verdicts explain each disposition. Print a clean report with header, characteristics table, and signature lines.
